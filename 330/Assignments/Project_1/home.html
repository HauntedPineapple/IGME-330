PokéFinder is a web app powered by PokéApi. You can search pokémon by name and filter results by various genres such as types and generations. If you leave the search field empty you can search all pokémon and use the filters to find the types of pokémon you want. The favorites page will allow you to store pokémon information for your convenience.

As of right now, the PokéFinder App only searches and displays pokémon from generations 1-7. Sword & Shield data has not yet been added.
